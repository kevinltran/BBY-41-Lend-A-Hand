MEET THE TEAM

What We Do

We are a group of First Year BCIT students working on this app to help during the COVID-19 pandemic. Working together has greatly increased our team work and collaboration skills as we need to adapt to each others' tendencies and schedules. Our team is hard-working and effiecient and we synergise well with each other. We covered each others weaknesses and provided effective assistance when needed. We hope our teamwork resonates throughout our app and that it is able to provide the help that you need.
